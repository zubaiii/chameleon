Gravity of Relationships

emphasizing
the power
of connection

Our inaugural venture into the world of 2D animation, "Gravity of Relationships" is a heartfelt exploration initiated by our team of artists. This visionary project aspires to merge artistic innovation with social consciousness, transcending conventional storytelling to delve into the profound domains of mental health, life challenges, and the transformative influence of familial bonds.

Storytelling. 2D Animation. Hand-drawn. Social Awareness.
