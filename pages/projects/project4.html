Devil inc

Revealing
humanity's
dark side.

Our team is diving into the world of animated storytelling with "Devil Inc" a project where we draw every frame by hand. We want to show how the devil influences our daily lives. From the initial idea to the final touches, our team is doing everything in-house. Imagine a devil affecting the choices we make every day. Our animated series explores this idea, making you think about how his influence is a part of our lives.

Storytelling. 2D Animation. Hand-drawn. Social Awareness.
